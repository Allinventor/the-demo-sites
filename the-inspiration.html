About The Inspiration

Have You Ever Heard of Stack Overflow?

If I go to as far back as I can remember, Stack Overflow was the only website with detailed questions and answers about anything i could think about when it relates to the internet or programming of any sorts.

Sure there are other websites that provide plenty of help such as W3Schools and SoloLearn. But there have been plenty of times when some of my questions are just too specific, or I just need a place for common re-occurring that I just need to copy and paste with different projects I'm testing out. As such, I decided it would be best to just do it myself and organize things in a rather un-common manner.

Thus, THE-DEMO-SITES was born!

Or at least I hope to do so. After all, this is the second domain I caught my hand on after accidentally losing the .com domain and buying the .dev as a replacement. Which is fine, but I don't really care as much. Considering that I have yet to decide whether to keep using Publii as the main CMS for this long-term project of mine.

I did decide on using GitHub as the hosting provider/main storage "hosting" solution. Though in reality I will be using CloudFlare as the actual Web Hosting provider due to the fact that they are just a better alternative when it comes to speed. Or at the very least I hope they are. I should (probably won't) double check if they are faster. At the very least they both have a relative free-solution to host a website. I don't wish to spend a lot of money on this and I would also like to keep all of this data public so that people can have access to this, should they ever need it, in what ever form they want.

The Plan

Do I have a plan?

I would like to say "I do".... but truth is, I have no idea what I am doing. Like 90% of adults. A statistic I just made up for the fun of it.

But, if I had to give a proper answer. I do.

First I will give a quick run-down of what I need to do. Then I will do them... next post about them. Finally, Repeat.
